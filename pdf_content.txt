Climate & Greenhouse Automation

Greenhouse Climate Control

Agro-Smart's climate control system ensures optimal growing conditions within

greenhouses, leveraging a network of integrated technologies.

Precision Sensors: Continuously monitor critical environmental factors like

temperature, humidity, and light intensity.

Automated Actuators: Regulate heating, cooling, and misting systems in real-time

based on sensor data.

Atmospheric Management: Control CO₂ enrichment, activate supplementary lighting,

and optimize ventilation for plant health.

This dynamic control prevents environmental stress, promotes vigorous growth, and

conserves energy.

An integrated system of sensors and actuators ensures a precisely

controlled environment for optimal crop yield.
